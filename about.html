How to Play

1

Start the Game

Click the "Start Game" button or press SPACE to begin your Flappy Bird adventure.

2

Control the Bird

Click your mouse or press SPACE to make the bird flap its wings and fly upward.

3

Avoid Obstacles

Navigate through the gaps between green pipes without touching them.

4

Score Points

Earn one point for each set of pipes you successfully pass through.
